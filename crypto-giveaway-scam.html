Crypto Giveaway Scams Explained

Crypto giveaway scams are fake promotions that promise free cryptocurrency in exchange for a small “verification” payment.

How Crypto Giveaway Scams Work

Scammers impersonate famous people or companies and claim they will double your crypto if you send funds first.

⚠️ Legit companies NEVER ask you to send crypto to receive more.

Common Red Flags

  • “Send 1 BTC, receive 2 BTC”
  • Fake celebrity endorsements
  • Urgent countdown timers
  • Anonymous wallet addresses

How to Stay Safe

Never send crypto to unknown wallets. Always verify promotions on official websites.
